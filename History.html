Brief History

The team, named "Thunderbolts FC", was founded in 2005 in a small town in England. In their first season, they earned promotion to the regional league. Over the next few years, the team continued to impress and earn promotions, eventually reaching the national league in 2012. In 2015, Thunderbolts FC won the national championship, marking the team's biggest accomplishment to date. In the following years, the team became a consistent top-performing club in the league, regularly competing in European competitions. In 2022, Thunderbolts FC relocated to a new, state-of-the-art stadium in the city to accommodate their growing fanbase.
